Influencers this is the New Wave! Get Involved! Engauge a new way to make your Influence Income BLOSSOM.

For years, digital influencers have had a single road. They’ve had to wait for brands to come to them and offer them a contract. They’ve also been limited to brands that approach them. While this model has worked massively well for some influencers, it has left other influencers high and dry.

But now, there’s a new road to digital influencer success. FluentBuzz is a digital influencer network that works the same way the other networks operate, with a couple of key differences.

The first difference is we let you, the influencer, pick the brands you want to work with. No more waiting for companies to come to you. You have your pick of what brands you want to promote to your followers.
Pick companies whose products you love and are enthusiastic about. This will keep you motivated and give your brand a highly excited member of their sales team.

The other big difference between FluentBuzz and our counterparts is our compensation model. As you bring customers into our platforms, you make a commission off every purchase they make. Any time they buy something from one of our brands, you get paid.

And that doesn’t just happen once. You’ll get paid every time a customer you bring to us makes a purchase from us for as long as that customer maintains a relationship with our brands. This is an opportunity to create repeat customers who pay out to you for years into the future. While it may take time to build a significant customer base, the possibility of long-term revenue is real.
