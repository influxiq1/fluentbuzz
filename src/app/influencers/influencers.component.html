Residual Income vs. Contracts

With the current influencer payout model, brands choose influencers based on the size of their following. The brand contacts the influencers, a contract is worked out, and a one-time fee is paid. And that’s that.

With a residual income model, like the one FluentBuzz uses, there is no up-front payout. But you will make a percentage of any purchase made by customers you bring to our sites. And this doesn’t just happen once. This happens for the life of the customer. For as long as your customers continue their relationships with our brands, you will make money off every single purchase they make.

This means that you can build a massive customer base that can pay out to you for years. If your follower buys a ticket or some merch from us five years down the road, you’ll get paid. If your follower buys a shirt or other gear from us ten years down the road, you’ll get paid. This is your chance to build a customer base that will provide long-term profit. An unending wellspring of revenue!

Tying You Into the
Larger Money

The ultimate goal of influencer marketing is simple: SALES. Brands pay influencers based on them promoting their products and increasing sales. And nothing makes brands more giddy than long-term, repeat customers.

That’s what Fluentbuzz is helping you do. We hook you up with the right brands so you can promote what you’re excited about and what you love. As you bring in customers and they make purchases, we pay you. Every time your followers buy from us, we pay you.

In fact, we pay you the way brands pay themselves. Every time someone buys something with a Star Wars or Nike or any other brand, the company behind the brand makes a little money. We take this concept and extend it to our influencers. When we get paid, you get paid. In that way, we’re tying you into a much larger potential money stream.

The name of the game here is brand loyalty. The more loyal your followers are to our brand, the more stuff they buy, the more you get paid. Customers who loyally purchase from one brand are like gold, and brands will pay you to find such customers. That’s how you find your way to the big money.
